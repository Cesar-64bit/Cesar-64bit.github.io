Cesar Bernal

Hello, my name is Cesar Oswaldo Bernal Sanchez. In this web I will talk a little about myself.

I became interested in computer science when I was in high school. My first programming language was C#. My curiosity led me to try C/C++ and I started doing competitive programming, solving many problems in the mentioned languages (you can check my profile on CodeChef). I enjoy data structures and algorithms, and I often read articles about them. Oh, and occasionally, I have written about data structures and algorithms too.
